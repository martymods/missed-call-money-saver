Community Platform Blueprint

Everything outlined in our original build brief now lives on this page. Visitors can browse freely, while members unlock deeper interactions powered by MongoDB, Stripe, Calendly, and trusted book APIs.

Open Access

Book lists, discussion archives, event highlights, and the merch shop remain open to the public. Curious readers can explore without friction, building a pipeline of future members.

Optional Profiles

Members register with email or social login. MongoDB stores avatars, bios, shelving lists, badges, and points so progress persists between visits.

Member Features

Logged-in readers comment, submit suggestions, track reading progress, and join events. Clear prompts encourage sign-ups exactly when engagement peaks.

Moderated Discussions

Book hubs host threaded conversations with moderation tools and alerts to keep the community supportive and safe.

Gamification & Rewards

Points System Badges Redeemable Rewards

Members earn points for finishing books, sharing reviews, attending events, and contributing to discussions. Milestones like Prolific Reader or Discussion Starter unlock badges, while point redemptions deliver merch discounts, digital exclusives, or honorary roles.

Monetization Mix

  • Affiliate Book Links: Trusted partner links appear on each book page with transparent messaging.
  • Membership Subscriptions: Monthly and annual tiers add perks such as exclusive guides, private forums, and discount codes.
  • Ticketed Events: Calendly embeds and Stripe Checkout simplify RSVPs for virtual and in-person gatherings.
  • Merchandise: Branded apparel, journals, and seasonal bundles tie into reward incentives.

Events & Scheduling

Inline Calendly widgets enable RSVP without leaving the site. Attendance writes back to MongoDB, updating point totals and unlocking badges like "Event Attendee."
