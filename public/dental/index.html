Frequently asked questions

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Yes. We operate on HIPAA-ready infrastructure, sign BAAs for paid customers, and provide audit logs plus granular permissions for your staff.

How do insurance checks work?

We run real-time eligibility (270/271) through authorized clearinghouses. When carriers lack APIs, we securely capture card images for manual follow-up without slowing your intake.

Can it integrate with my current systems?

Yes. We connect with Calendly out of the box and bridge to leading practice management systems (Dentrix, Open Dental, Eaglesoft) via webhook sync. Custom integrations are available on Growth and Enterprise.

Will my team stay in the loop?

Absolutely. Every call, SMS, email, insurance response, and scheduling action appears in your dashboard with transcripts, so your team can jump in any time.
